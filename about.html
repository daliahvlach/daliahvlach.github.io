About

Swiss born Daliah Vlach studied Interior Design as a second carrier path at KLC school of design at London’s Chelsea Harbor.

After a successful Diploma graduation she was employed for several years at high-end property development firm Candy & Candy in London where amongst others she designed and completed the residential property La Belle Epoque in Monaco. Daliah established her own company in Nairobi in 2009 and since then has successfully implemented several high-end residential as well as commercial projects.

Although Daliah adjusts her design style to suit the locally available materials and craftsmanship, she sees every project as a new challenge to find the right solutions for the clients and their properties.

“I like to believe that every client and every property is different and therefore every project needs its own individual approach. Client’s dreams, practicality aspects and budgets as well as the property’s architecture and its surroundings need to be considered in order to achieve a satisfying solution. I always try to aim at the perfect result in every aspect so that my clients never feel they had to compromise.”

Daliah Vlach
