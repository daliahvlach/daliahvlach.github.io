Craftmanship

At DV Interiors we take great pride in working together with our immensely talented Kenyan craftsmen and women.

While at times we have to bring materials or support from abroad, we try and create most of our interior fit-outs here locally.

Our carpenters produce incredibly beautiful floors and doors, skillfully crafted joinery and fantastic furniture from our African timbers. All our curtains, cushions and throws are professionally hand stitched by our Kenyan seamstresses and individually created for each home.

We try and push the boundaries of local knowledge and exposure, challenging our teams to grow with us and reach new limits with every creation and every new project.
