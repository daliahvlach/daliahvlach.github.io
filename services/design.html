Design

The whole design process is very clearly documented, so that the client, as well as all contractors involved know exactly what needs to be done at all times.

We invest a lot of time in planning all the details of a project, from the initial property research, space planning, abstract mood concept, through to the sketches, drawings and material choices. We believe that a well planned project avoids a lot of problems on site, saves time and unnecessary expenses, and makes sure, that the clients expectations are met.

From there we follow through by closely overlooking the site implementation, the manufacturing process of bespoke built joinery and furniture as well as the production of custom made soft furnishings. Initial Client meeting and brief Concept Proposal with sketches and visuals Detailed Drawing packages, FF&E packages and presentation of all finishes and fabrics. Procurement and Project Management for site implementation. Finishing and dressing of the property
